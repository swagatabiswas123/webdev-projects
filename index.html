SEC. 01 | Why Mangalyaan

The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), also called Mangalyaan is a space probe orbiting Mars since 24 September 2014.

mangalyaan

On November 5, 2013, the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) launched its first spacecraft bound for Mars.

India built Mangalyaan (“Mars craft” in English) to study the Red Planet and test key technologies required for exploring the inner solar system. The Mangalyaan spacecraft successfully entered Mars orbit on September 23, 2014, making ISRO only the fourth space agency in the world to do so.

Mangalyaan has been operating for more than seven years now, observing Martian landscapes and studying their composition using its five science instruments.
